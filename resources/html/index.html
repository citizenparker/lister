Lister is the world's simplest way to create and share lists of things! Really! I know everyone says this, but Lister means it from the bottom of its heart! So simple, it's stupid!

Let's Get Listing!

1. Start that List!

Click that beautiful button up there to start on your own wonderful list! Once you're there, you can add, complete, and clear things off that list! OH YEAH!

2. Share that List!

Sharing a list couldn't be easier - just send the URL to someone! Yeah, that's it! No accounts, no security, no anything! The URLs are a little obscure so that you can't guess it, but that's it! So don't store the launch codes on Lister, that would be a bad idea!

3. Never lose that URL!

How do you get to a list without the URL? You don't! So you should probably bookmark it if you plan on coming back to it in the future!
